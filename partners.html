    • PARTNERS

    Partnerships are the key in how Sanghamitra operates. We partner with creative individuals and organizations with shared interests and complementary strengths. Depending on the business line, we might develop and deliver products, provide strategy and technology consulting services, or invest in developing infrastructure. The following are our current partners and we’re actively working on bringing more on board.
