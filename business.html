    • BUSINESS LINES




    We have interests in a wide range of business areas including consumer products, artworks, and technology. The following are our current business lines and we are actively working on expanding into new areas. Our consumer products are available online via the store website





    CLOTHING LINE


    Starting with masks to protect against COVID-19, our clothing and accessories line now also includes several styles of handbags and purses. More products are in the works.






    ARTWORKS

    Our art frames feature works by individual artists and collaborations, and include paintings, sketches, digital art and calligraphy. We are looking to partner with a wide range of artists.



    TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS


    We offer strategy consulting and product development services in learning platforms, data science and machine learning. We partner with organizations in addition to having our own in-house development.
